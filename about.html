About Us

Dost Trust is a is a Public Charitable Trust is made for imparting education, health, rural development, environmental, sports, art and culture, under the name and style of Dost Trust Jamshedpur. Dost trust also aims to implement an Old Age Home, Hospital and a School for the needy.

Our Mission

The MISSION is to provide quality health care and educational facilities to all at affordable cost with love and compassion in the most friendly environment.We aim to benefit all social elements, with a special emphasis on the economically weaker section.

Our Vision

To become a center of excellence in healthcare, education and nourishing comparable with world standards with personalized approach at affordable cost with high standard of ethics, love and compassion.
