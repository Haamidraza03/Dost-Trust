Trust Certificate

This is a Public Charitable Trust is made for imparting education, health, rural development, environmental, sports, art and culture, under the name and style of Dost Trust Jamshedpur. Dost trust also aims to implement an Old Age Home, Hospital and a School for the needy.

Become a Member

The membership of "Dost Trust" is open to all Community minority communities and other OBC community residing at Jamshedpur or Jharkhand or all India, thereby on payment of the prescribed subscription and admission fee together with an application in prescribed forms object to approval by the board of trustee managing committee. The membership fee is Rs.100 and annual subscription is Rs.600 provided he/she agrees to abide by rules and bylaws that may be made by "Dost Trust" from time to time.

Our Aims and Objectives

1. To provide support and guidance to students from economically deprived through scholarships and sponsorship programmes

2. To provide financial support for education of poor/underprivileged individual,group and society members by giving notebooks/books/fees/ scholarship etc. from fund resources

3. To help widow an older dependent person using microfinance

4. To make arrangements, of funeral ceremony for the deceased members of poor family

5. To organise various lectures by the various professionals such as doctors, lawyers, professional various fields

6. To provide medical hospital nursing home care services Babycare services and women healthcare services

7. To undertake all types of projects for establishment and running or management of health centres such as hospital and health related centres like pharmacy centre, pathological centre, medical related library etc

8. To promote and encourage physical fitness programme and moral education to children, youth and women by various fitness trainer and motivational speakers

9. To provide farming and animal husbandry and to provide agricultural machineries and training program for farmers

10. Two eradicate social evils like dowry, drug addiction and other using various seminars cultural program, program by social reformers and motivational program
